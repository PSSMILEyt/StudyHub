About Exam Aagadi

👤 About This App

Exam Aagadi by PSSMILE is a complete learning platform designed specially for SEE and Class 10 students in Nepal. It provides well-organized notes, video explanations, and revision materials to help students understand concepts clearly and prepare effectively for exams.

📚 What You Get
• Subject-wise structured notes
• Easy video explanations
• Important questions & exercises
• Quick revision materials

🎯 Our Mission
To make education simple, free, and accessible for every student in Nepal.
